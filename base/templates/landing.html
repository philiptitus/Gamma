About Gamma

Gamma is an advanced emotion and mood analyzer that uses video recordings to provide in-depth emotional insights. Through our unique blend of AI and machine learning, Gamma detects and monitors emotional shifts, calmness levels, and stress indicators, creating a comprehensive emotional analysis tailored for each user.

Core Features
  • Video Emotion Analysis: Identifies emotions in real-time, including happiness, calmness, and stress.
  • Emotion Trends & Stability: Visualizes emotional changes, displaying trends and assessing stability over time.
  • Stress & Fatigue Detection: Detects stress and fatigue through facial and eye pattern analysis.
  • Token-Based Session Tracking: Each session is assigned a unique token for easy tracking of individual analyses.
  • AWS S3 Integration: Enables efficient video storage and processing using Amazon S3.
  • Email Reporting: Sends detailed reports with dominant emotions, stability scores, and calmness percentages to users.
Technologies Used
  • Python Libraries: Leverages Django, OpenCV, DeepFace, and TensorFlow for performance-optimized analysis.
  • AWS S3 Storage: Efficiently manages and streams video files for large-scale analyses.
  • React Native (mobile app coming soon)
